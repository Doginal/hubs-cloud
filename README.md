## What is Hubs Cloud?

Hubs Cloud creates and manages all of the AWS resources needed to host your own immersive spaces from your company or organizationâ€™s own account. Use your own domain or use Route53 to create a new site, running on a single EC2 instance or scaled up to multiple servers for greater system-wide concurrency. Easily customize your platform with your own branding, upload your own 3D content, or select from the vast array of avatars and scenes licensed under Creative Commons for the Hubs platform. Now available to deploy on offered on DigitalOcean (alpha).
